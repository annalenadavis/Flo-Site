    Meet Stefan

    Stefan Ewing is a Pennsylvania native, with two decades of study in martial arts, and over 10 years of teaching experience. He achieved the rank of 3rd degree black belt in Tang Soo Do (Korean Karate) and is now an Amateur kickboxing/Muay Thai fighter. Stefan moved to San Diego in 2014, and has spent his time since then teaching kickboxing and self-defense privately and refining his craft under Kru Carl Gebhardt at Steel MMA San Diego. He now lives in Pacific Beach and devotes his time to training and teaching martial arts.
